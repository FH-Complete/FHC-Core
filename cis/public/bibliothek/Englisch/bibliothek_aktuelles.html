E-mail-Reminders 13.04.2010
As of December 2009, students who have borrowed media are automatically sent an e-mail reminder shortly before the due date as well as when their media has become overdue. Users who fail to return borrowed media within 7 days of the due date will be blocked from borrowing further media. At the same time, late fees shall apply. The late fee shall be € 4 if paid within 14 days after being blocked from further borrowing, and € 7 after that date. In your own interest, we therefore ask you to apply for extensions (by phone or e-mail) in due time. Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact the library team.
