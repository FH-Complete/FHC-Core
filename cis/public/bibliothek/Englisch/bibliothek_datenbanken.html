Database-Infosystem (DBIS)
DBIS is a cooperative service developed by the Regensburg University Library for the use of scientific databases.
DBIS provides free online access to more than 3,000 scientific databases from a variety of fields covering everything from computer science bibliographies to environmental databases and even online dictionaries for economics.

It is also possible to use DBIS to access the "ScienceDirect College Edition" for which the Technikum has purchased a license.
