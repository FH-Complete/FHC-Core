 Important Information

Diploma Theses
Since the diploma theses are located in a separate room on the 5th floor, they may only be viewed by appointment. To search for a diploma thesis from a particular course of study in the online catalog, please enter the name of the course of study and the word dipl* in the search field. Diploma theses are designated with the abbreviation DA after the reference number. You may request a maximum of five works by sending an e-mail to bibliothek@technikum-wien.at listing the title(s) you would like to borrow. You can retrieve the works from the library on the following day and may borrow them for a maximum of 1 month. Thank you for your cooperation!
